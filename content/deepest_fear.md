My deepest fear is not that these systems have no promise.<br></br>
My deepest fear is that these systems are rich beyond measure.<br></br>
It is the potential, not lack thereof, that most frightens me.<br></br>

I ask myself, who am I to make something Beautiful and True,<br></br>
Actually, who am I not to?<br></br>
I am an experiencer of our Universe, and the creator of my own,<br></br>

My playing small does not serve thew world.<br></br>
There is nothing enlightened about shirking away from the light to protect my ego.<br></br>

I am meant to explore, as children do.<br></br>
I am here to seek the Beauty and Truth that exists in the Universe.<br></br>

It's not just in these systems and games. It's everywhere.<br></br>

And as I seek that here, it allows us all to witness something pure.<br></br>
And as we approach the Truth, our presence automatically liberate others.<br></br>
<br></br>

_Liberally plagarized_ from [Marianne Williamson](https://www.poemist.com/marianne-williamson/our-deepest-fear)
